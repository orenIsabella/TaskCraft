Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of input does TaskCraft understand?

TaskCraft understands natural language! Just type like you're texting a friend. Mention dates, times, people, and what you need to do.

Does TaskCraft work offline?

The web app requires an internet connection for AI processing. However, your calendar syncs to your device and works offline.

Can I edit events after they're created?

Yes! Events sync to your calendar where you can edit them using your calendar app of choice.

How do I provide feedback on results?

Visit the History page and use the thumbs up/down buttons on each event. This helps improve accuracy over time!

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and never share your personal information. View our privacy policy in Settings.
